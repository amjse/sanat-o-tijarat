Introduction

Who We Are

Sanat-O-Tijarat (Commerce & Trade) is a national auxiliary department of the Ahmadiyya Muslim Jamaat Sweden, dedicated to supporting and empowering members in their professional and entrepreneurial pursuits — rooted in Islamic values of honesty, excellence, and mutual benefit.

Our department serves as a bridge between faith and profession, helping Ahmadi businesspeople, entrepreneurs, professionals, and students in Sweden connect, grow, and contribute positively to Swedish society and the broader economy.

We organise workshops, mentorship initiatives, networking events, and training programmes designed to foster professional excellence and ethical business conduct in line with the teachings of Islam Ahmadiyyat.

Inspiration & Guidance

Messages of Guidance

Hazrat Musleh Maud (RA)

Second Caliph of the Ahmadiyya Muslim Community

"Engage in trade and commerce with full trust in Allah and with the highest degree of honesty. Do not let the pursuit of wealth distract you from your spiritual duties, nor let your faith be an excuse to neglect your worldly responsibilities."

Hazrat Musleh Maud (RA) consistently emphasised that Ahmadis must distinguish themselves in every field of life — including commerce, industry, and trade — as ambassadors of true Islamic values. He urged members to become economically self-sufficient so they could better serve the Jamaat and humanity.

Hazrat Mirza Tahir Ahmad (RA)

Khalifatul Masih Al-Rabi' — Friday Sermon, 15 April 1983

In his landmark sermon on excelling in all fields including Sanat-O-Tijarat, Hazrat Khalifatul Masih IV (RA) called upon all Ahmadi Muslims to pursue excellence in commerce, trade, and craftsmanship as a religious and communal obligation.

He outlined that the progress of the Jamaat was deeply linked to the economic and professional advancement of its members, and that engaging in honest trade was itself a form of worship. This sermon remains a foundational reference for our department's mission.

National Secretary's Message

Message from the National Secretary — Sanat-O-Tijarat Sweden

Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatuhu,

Dear brothers and sisters, professionals, entrepreneurs, and students — it is with great humility and gratitude to Allah that I serve as National Secretary of Sanat-O-Tijarat Sweden. This department exists not merely as an organisational unit, but as a living expression of our commitment to excellence in all spheres of life.

Sweden has provided us a remarkable environment to build, create, and contribute. Our responsibility is to honour this opportunity by embodying the highest standards of professional ethics and business integrity — as taught by our beloved Huzoor and the foundational guidance of our Khulafa.

I invite every Ahmadi professional, business owner, student, and entrepreneur in Sweden to connect with us, share your expertise, and join our growing network. Together, through commerce rooted in faith, we can be a shining example for the broader Swedish society.

Nosherwan Adil
National Secretary, Sanat-O-Tijarat

Foundational Guidance

Landmark Sermon on Commerce & Excellence

Khutbat-e-Tahir · Volume 2

Friday Sermon on Excelling in All Fields Including Sanat-O-Tijarat

Hazrat Mirza Tahir Ahmad (RA) · 15 April 1983 · Pages 217–228

In this landmark sermon, Hazrat Khalifatul Masih IV (RA) delivered a comprehensive vision for Ahmadi Muslims to excel in commerce, trade, and craft — not merely as a worldly pursuit, but as a divine obligation. He outlined that economic strength of the Jamaat was inseparable from its spiritual mission.

He called on members to pursue professional and business excellence as an act of worship, to engage in ethical trade, to avoid all forms of dishonesty, and to contribute their skills and resources to the betterment of society.
